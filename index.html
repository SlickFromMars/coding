TSA Coding II

What is Coding?

In coding, you will be tested on your knowledge of various coding languages through an exam. You can study and prepare for this all year in order to score well. If you score higher than enough teams, you will make it to semifinals. When your team of 2 makes it to semifinals, you will participate in a coding challenge. This usually consists of 5 prompts that you need to follow, and program, in the language of your choice.
